Our Departments

Our hospital offers comprehensive healthcare services through specialized departments equipped with modern technology and experienced medical professionals.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides expert diagnosis and treatment for heart-related diseases. We use advanced technology to ensure the best cardiac care for patients.

Services

  • ECG
  • Heart Checkup
  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Heart Surgery Consultation

Neurology

The Neurology Department specializes in treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system with modern medical facilities.

Services

  • Migraine Treatment
  • Stroke Care
  • Epilepsy Treatment
  • Brain Disorder Diagnosis

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedic Department provides treatment for bone, joint, muscle, and spine-related problems with expert surgeons and advanced equipment.

Services

  • Joint Replacement
  • Spine Care
  • Sports Injury Treatment

Pediatrics
